ABOUT ME!


After nearly 15 years of the grueling local news grind, my wife and I decided it was time for a change. So we sold our house and most of our stuff and moved to Asheville, North Carolina for a computer coding boot-camp. After seven long months of searching across the country for coding work, we finally decided to branch out on our own and try to make a go of it. Now we find ourselves in Flagstaff, Arizona.

I bring with me my fast, efficient, and effective video editing skills; my raw, untrained, but impressive photography skills; and my elegant, affordable, but powerful web design skills.

My wife and I have absolutely fallen in love with FLG and we hope to make our permanent home here. Let us help you make this town as awesome as we all know it can be!
